Santiago L.

Barcelona, Spain | Abstract Artist

Artist Statement

"My work explores the dynamic interplay between color and emotion. I aim to create a visceral response from the viewer, capturing moments of energy and quiet contemplation through bold strokes and a vibrant palette."

Born in Valencia and now based in Barcelona, Santiago L. has been immersed in the world of abstract art since his early twenties. His travels across Southern Europe have deeply influenced his use of warm, earthy tones contrasted with vibrant, unexpected splashes of color.

He believes that art should not just be seen, but felt. Each piece is a journey, a non-verbal dialogue between the canvas and the observer.
